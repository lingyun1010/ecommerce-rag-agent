# Policy

Source URL: https://muraritual.com

## Shipping

MÙRA states that it ships to Australia, New Zealand, US, and UK. The site also states "worldwide shipping" in banner text. Shipping rates are calculated at checkout.

Free shipping is stated as available on orders over $50 AUD within Australia & NZ.

## Returns

MÙRA has a 30-day return policy. Customers have 30 days after receiving an item to request a return.

To be eligible for a return, the item must be in the same condition it was received: unworn or unused, with tags, in its original packaging, and with a receipt or proof of purchase.

Returns must be requested before sending items back. Items sent back without first requesting a return will not be accepted.

Return address listed on the policy page: U702, 2 Murrell St. Ashfield Sydney 2131.

For used shampoo bars, the FAQ states that physical returns cannot be accepted for hygiene reasons, but customers are encouraged to reach out for help finding a solution.

## Refunds

MÙRA states that once a return is received and inspected, customers will be notified whether the refund is approved.

If approved, the refund is automatically issued to the original payment method within 10 business days. The site notes that banks or credit card companies may take additional time to process and post the refund.

If more than 15 business days have passed since an approved return, the refund policy asks customers to contact zhaolingyun1010@gmail.com.

## Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days.

Delivery time varies by location, but the FAQ states that delivery usually takes 2-6 business days within Australia.

## Exchanges

The refund policy states that the fastest way to ensure the customer gets the item they want is to return the current item, and once the return is accepted, make a separate purchase for the new item.

## Cancellations

For merchandise shipped into the European Union, the policy states that customers have the right to cancel or return an order within 14 days, for any reason and without justification, provided the item is in the same condition received: unworn or unused, with tags, in its original packaging, and with receipt or proof of purchase.

Other cancellation rules: Not specified

## Contact

## Non-returnable Items

The refund policy states that certain items cannot be returned, including perishable goods, custom products, personal care goods, hazardous materials, flammable liquids, gases, sale items, and gift cards.

Customers are asked to get in touch with questions or concerns about a specific item.

## Damaged, Defective, or Wrong Items

Customers should inspect orders upon reception and contact MÙRA immediately if an item is defective, damaged, or wrong, so the issue can be evaluated and made right.

## 30-Day Peace of Mind

For first-time purchases, MÙRA states that if the first ritual does not feel right, customers can reach out within 30 days of delivery. The site says MÙRA will take time to understand the customer's experience and offer a thoughtful solution, which may be a replacement, store credit, or refund.
